Places to go

Autumn is a fantastic time to be in New England. We hope you stay a while to enjoy the surrounding area.

Close to us

Five minutes from our house is Massasoit State Park. Trails will take you around the many ponds and Lake Rico. It gets busy, but most people walk up the road through the middle of the park and the trails usually stay pretty empty. We go there all the time, and love it.

Slightly further afield is Betty's Neck. It's beautiful and off the beaten path (literally). Take a left off near 57 Long Point Rd, drive along the dirt road past the nursing home keeping the cranberry bogs to your left and the lake to your right. Drive into the wooded area and you'll find parking there.

It will be pick your own apple season! If you go to Betty's Neck, Keith's farm and Perry Hill Orchard are nearby. Close to that is our absolute favourite ice cream place - Country Whip. Their vanilla homemade soft serve is the best (kiddie size= UK small). If you have kids, you might also want to check out some corn mazes & hayrides

Further away

It's probably a little early to catch the beautiful "fall foliage" around us - so we highly recommend extending your time and taking a trip north to Vermont, New Hampshire, or Maine. If you have a little longer, Acadia National Park is amazing and the only national park in the North East.

Closer by, Cape Cod is famous for beautiful beaches and whale watching.

If you're more enticed by the city, Boston is only an hour away, and New York City is 4 hours by car, or 4-5 hrs by train from either Boston or Providence.
